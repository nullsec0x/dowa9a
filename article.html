Welcome back! You've heard the vision, you've seen the momentum, but now it's time to meet the true architects of our sound. At DOWA9A, we don't just release music; we forge new sonic territories. The music that drives us forward—the tracks that define the next wave of Arabic sound—start with the unparalleled talent you're about to meet. These individuals don't follow trends; they set the pace. They're the ones up late, turning raw ideas into absolute fire. Get ready to salute the backbone of our label. Please give a huge welcome to the incredible producers of DOWA9A...


ussef777

Ussef777 is a Moroccan music producer known for creating innovative beats that fuse electronic vibes, hip-hop energy, and cinematic melodies. With a passion for sound design and storytelling, he transforms raw ideas into tracks that move both the mind and the body.

Driven by creativity and a vision to inspire, Ussef777 is building his own signature style — one that pushes boundaries while staying true to authentic emotion. Whether in the studio or online, his music reflects dedication, originality, and a deep love for he craft.

luca mus!c

LUCA MUS!C seems to be an emerging music producer and artist known for his unique sound, blending hip-hop energy with cinematic melodies. At just 19 years old, he's already made a name for himself with two notable projects: "VISION INFINIE" and "THE WAR WITHIN". These projects showcase his creativity and passion for music, featuring collaborations with other artists and available on all major streaming platforms.

Antoriquo

Antoriquo is the producer alias of Moroccan artist Yassine Mohsini, based in Casablanca (Bourgogne). His sound fuses dark boom bap, abstract hip-hop, house punk, and experimental textures, shaping raw underground atmospheres that resist the mainstream. Under the name Yung P, he steps to the mic, rapping with the same unfiltered energy and grit found in his beats. Together, Antoriquo and Yung P reflect two sides of one vision pushing Moroccan hip-hop and alternative sounds forward with honesty, rebellion, and originality.

cxlltur

Cxlltur, a music producer, beatmaker, and loopmaker from Meknes, Morocco. I started making beats in 2022, first experimenting with FL Studio Mobile, and since then I've been developing my own style.

My sound is a mix of experimental, electronic, retro, lofi, emotional, and hip-hop influences, shaped by artists such as Kanye West, Tyler the Creator, Frank Ocean, Radiohead, and Moroccan pioneers like Madd, Shobee, MW, and Keight.

He've worked with artists including Badfox, Zeno, Sadluxe, and Zack, with upcoming unreleased projects with Tahades, Veintiuno, and Ayuur. My main goal is to elevate Moroccan hip-hop production and become one of the leading producers both in Morocco and worldwide.

moun*

moun* is a young multi media artist that is mainly focused on music originated from Casablanca and now based in Paris, they also makes music under the name pinkshell*. They started making music back in 2020 when they started learning music production and then they kept developing their sound from rap to rnb to punk, but in 2025 they finally found their own sound with the song (monkey on ketamine*). They describe the sound as coherent chaos, to get to this sound they took inspiration from multiple artists from different genres like (jazz, post punk, hyperpop, emo, rnb, alt rock). In their music moun* try to talk about topics that matter to our generation like phone addiction, politics, gender identity, and they focus a lot on the overall anxiety that most of our generation feel with all the problems going on in the world right now. They're debut ep under the name of moun* (body moving through a screen*) is dropping soon and it's a really creative and experimental peace of art that really needs all our attention, moun* is definitely one of the artists you should be following.
